Fur Trade Days 2022

Join the City of Chadron and surrounding communities for the 46th annual Fur Trade Days celebration July 7-10, 2022! Fur Trade Days was also selected as Northern Panhandle's Best Community Festival two years in a row - 2014 and 2015.

We can't wait, see you in Chadron!

Mac's Carnival

Fur Trade Days is excited to partner with Mac’s Carnival again for 2022!
 

Entertainment!

Fur Trade Days is excited to bring you great entertainment downtown on Friday and Saturday nights!
 

Fireworks

Fur Trade Days' 2022 Fireworks is tentatively planned for Friday night, more details here as they become available.
 

Traders' Market

The Chadron Fur Trade Days Traders' Market features merchants from around the area and across the country, and provides something for everyone. The 46th annual attraction is planned to remain at the crowd-favorite Dawes County Courthouse Lawn and boasts a slate of new merchants as well as familiar favorites!
 

Lions Club FTD Parade

The 46th Annual Lions Club Fur Trade Days Parade starts at 10 a.m. on Saturday, July 9 and proceeds down Main Street from Tenth to First streets. Candy and other goodies will be passed out along the parade route. Come early to claim a good seat and enjoy the excitement!
 

Activities and More

Family-friendly fun begins Friday at the Dawes County Court House Gazebo.
 

Buckskinner's Camp

The Bordeaux Creek Fur Trade and Muzzleloading Association’s Rendezvous, located 3.5 miles east and 8 miles south of Chadron, is expected to open Thursday, July 7, for camp setup.
