Entertainment!

Fur Trade Days is excited to bring you great entertainment downtown on Friday and Saturday nights!

Devon Worley Band @ Bands on Bordeaux — Thursday, 6 - 9 p.m. Railroad Park, First & Main streets

Chadron Chamber of Commerce’s Bands on Bordeaux kicks off the week’s events with a Thursday night performance

Alexa Winston — Thursday, 9 p.m. - Midnight Downtown Plaza, Second & Main streets

Raised in Chadron, Winston has played acoustic Americana, country and folk favorites as well as original compositions across the regional music scenes for more than a decade and is thrilled to be part of this year’s Fur Trade Days.

 

Brianna Helbling — Friday, 9 p.m. - 1 a.m. Downtown, Second St & Chadron Ave

Brianna brings her best in Country/Rock/Pop/Originals solo and with her full band.

 

Pete Butler — Saturday, Noon, Courthouse Gazebo Fourth & Main streets

Chadron-area farrier, Pete Butler performs old-time country.

 

Johnny Cochran — Saturday, 9 p.m. - 1 a.m. Downtown, Second St & Chadron Ave

Johnny Cochran is a guitar slinging country pop artist from South Louisiana.
