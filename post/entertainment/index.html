Entertainment!

Fur Trade Days is excited to bring you great entertainment downtown. This year features bands out of the Black Hills, Flannel on Friday and Judd Hoos Saturday nights!

Tim Zach @ Bands on Bordeaux — Thursday, 6 - 9 p.m. Railroad Park, First & Main streets

Chadron Chamber of Commerce’s Bands on Bordeaux kicks off the week’s events with a Thursday night performance

Alexa — Thursday, 9 p.m. - Midnight Downtown Plaza, Second & Main streets

Hometown favorite, Alexa Winston returns to Chadron for some later entertainment Thursday night. Bonus: Your $10 commemorative 2023 Fur Trade Days cup allows you to enjoy a drink outside during this show at Downtown Plaza.

 

Flannel — Friday, 9 p.m. - 1 a.m. Downtown, Second & Main streets

Home grown from the Black Hills of South Dakota, Flannel is a Pop/Rock/Funk cover band playing a wide range of 90’s hits.

 

Judd Hoos — Saturday, 9 p.m. - 1 a.m. Downtown, Second & Main streets

Returning Fur Trade Days favorite, Judd Hoos is an independent, American rock band from the Black Hills of South Dakota.
