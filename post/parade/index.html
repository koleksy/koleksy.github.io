Lions Club FTD Parade

The 44th Annual Lions Club Fur Trade Days Parade starts at 10 a.m. on Saturday, July 11 and proceeds down Main Street from Tenth to First streets. Candy and other goodies will be passed out along the parade route. Come early to claim a good seat and enjoy the excitement!
