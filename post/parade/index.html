Lions Club FTD Parade

The 46th Annual Lions Club Fur Trade Days Parade starts at 10 a.m. on Saturday, July 9 and proceeds down Main Street from Tenth to First streets. Candy and other goodies will be passed out along the parade route. Come early to claim a good seat and enjoy the excitement!

Get the registration form for the Lions Club Parade right here! The theme of this year’s parade is “Rocking USA.” Participants must check in by 9 a.m. at the Lions Club Kiosk on 10th Street for line-up. Parade begins at 10 a.m.

 

Before the Parade:

Historic Flag Parade Presentation

Fur Trade Days is thrilled to announce the return of the historic flags of the fur trade to this year’s celebration. Flags will be presented with narrated facts and history prior to the parade (about 9:30 a.m.) in the 400 block of Main Street, in front of the Dawes County Court House.

Parade of Puppies

FTD’s Parade of Puppies is a fun new event for the whole family just before the big Parade!

The Parade of Puppies is set to run just before the big parade takes off, (about 9:45 a.m.) in the 400 block of Main Street, in front of the Dawes County Court House. Entrants’ will be introduced with their dogs as they walk up the block.

No pre-registration is required for the Parade of Puppies. Please bring your dog (or dogs!) to the courthouse steps to fill out a card with your name, dog’s name, and a fun fact.
