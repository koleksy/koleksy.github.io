Buckskinner's Camp

The Bordeaux Creek Fur Trade and Muzzleloading Association’s Rendezvous, located 3.5 miles east and 8 miles south of Chadron, is expected to open Thursday, July 8, for camp setup.

Details for the Primitive Rendezvous during this year’s Fur Trade Days have been finalized and more information is available from this form. For more information, contact Norman Martin at (308)432-3474 or marbow@bbc.net

The camp is always open to the public, but Friday, July 9, 9 a.m. – 7 p.m., is the best time to catch the major activities. Enjoy muzzleloading shoots, tomahawk and knife throwing contests, archery, and plenty of carousing afterward. Check out the fur, handicrafts, bead work and other period items offered for sale by traders.
