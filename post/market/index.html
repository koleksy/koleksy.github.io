Traders' Market

From food to finds, antiques and oddities, the Fur Trade Days Traders' Market features food vendors and craft merchants from around the region and across the country; providing something of interest for just about everyone.

2023 Traders' Market

The Fur Trade Days Traders’ Market is an outdoor, rain or shine event. Hours are noon to 7 p.m., Friday, July 14 and 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. Saturday, July 15. Vendor operation on Friday is optional. However, vendors must be set up prior to 9 a.m. Saturday to avoid conflict with other events and road closures
