Traders' Market

The Chadron Fur Trade Days Traders’ Market features merchants from around the area and across the country, and provides something for everyone. The 45th annual attraction is planned to remain at the crowd-favorite Dawes County Courthouse Lawn and boasts a slate of new merchants as well as familiar favorites!

Download the application for the 2021 Traders’ Market

2020 Reserved Vendors

Traders Market vendors who have already reserved spots in the 2020 Traders Market will be offered the option to roll their reservation to 2021. Vendors can contact Carly Schnell at familyevents@furtradedays.com.

Confirmed 2021 vendors

Teddy Bear Mobile Central Nebraska

Choose your teddy, stuff it, take it home, and love it!

Stitching Aswesome, Alliance, Nebraska

Handmade bags at affordable prices direct from Alliance, Nebraska! Stitching Awesome Shop launched in December of 2019, when a workplace injury turned a creative hobby into a new revenue stream. Large, medium, and small bags and purses, drink totes, and more!

Rosita’s Trokita

From Scotts Bluff, Nebraska’s own Rosita’s Restaurant comes the food truck Rosita’s Trokita.

The food truck offers a spin-off menu of their restaurant fare. Enjoy puffy tacos, smothered burritos, panchos, and enchiladas!
